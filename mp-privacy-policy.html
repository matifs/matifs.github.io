Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 13, 2025

Thank you for using Video Player. This Privacy Policy describes how our app handles user information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal or sensitive user data.

The app operates completely offline and does not transmit any data to our servers.

2. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage access – to read and display videos stored on your device.
  • Network access – required only for showing ads (no personal data is collected).
  • Picture-in-Picture permission – to allow you to continue watching videos in a small window while using other apps.

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the video player and never for collecting or tracking user information.

3. Advertising

The app includes advertisements provided by third-party networks such as Google AdMob. These networks may use cookies or device identifiers to provide relevant ads.

For details on how Google uses data, visit: Google’s Partner Policy.

4. Picture-in-Picture (PiP) Mode

The app supports Android’s native Picture-in-Picture mode. This feature is handled entirely on your device and does not involve data collection, tracking, or recording.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of your personal information being stored, shared, or exposed.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect improvements or legal changes. Any updates will appear on this page with a new revision date.
